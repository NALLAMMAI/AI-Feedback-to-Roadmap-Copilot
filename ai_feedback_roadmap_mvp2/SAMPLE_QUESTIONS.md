# Sample PM Questions for the RAG Evidence Base

Use these in the **RAG Evidence Q&A** tab:

1. What should we build first for enterprise SRE and DevOps teams, and why?
2. Which themes are most tied to sales or revenue impact?
3. What evidence supports building an AI incident timeline feature?
4. Compare alert deduplication vs AI root-cause suggestions. Which should be prioritized first?
5. What are the biggest pain points for on-call engineers?
6. Which enterprise blockers appear most often in sales notes?
7. What product gaps are competitors using to differentiate?
8. Which roadmap items should be validated before building?
9. What are the risks of adding AI-generated root-cause analysis?
10. Draft an MVP scope for reducing incident triage time.
